## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to `https://careers.microsoft.com/`.
2. Use the search bar or browse by profession, location, or team.
3. Click on a job listing to view details.
4. Click "Apply" to begin the application.
5. Sign in with a Microsoft account (or create one).
6. LinkedIn profile import is available (Microsoft owns LinkedIn).
7. Upload resume and fill out the application form.
8. Complete screening questions.
9. Submit the application.

## Known Quirks

- Microsoft uses a custom career portal with LinkedIn integration.
- Microsoft account required for application.
- "Apply with LinkedIn" auto-fills many fields.
- Some roles have team-specific screening questions.
- Microsoft is one of the largest H-1B sponsors in the US.
- The portal has a built-in application tracker.
