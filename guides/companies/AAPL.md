## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to `https://jobs.apple.com/`.
2. Use the search bar to find positions by keyword and location.
3. Click on a job listing to view the detail page.
4. Click "Apply" to begin the application.
5. Apple may require Apple ID sign-in.
6. Follow the multi-step application form.
7. Upload resume when prompted.
8. Complete screening questions.
9. Submit the application.

## Known Quirks

- Apple uses a fully custom career portal, not a third-party ATS.
- Apple ID may be required to apply.
- The site is highly polished but can be slow to load.
- Job search supports filtering by team, location, and job type.
- Some roles are only posted internally.
