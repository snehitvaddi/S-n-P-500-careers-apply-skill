## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to the HPE Workday career site.
2. Search for positions by keyword and location.
3. Click on a job listing.
4. Click "Apply" to start the Workday wizard.
5. Create a Workday account or sign in.
6. Follow the standard Workday 7-step wizard.
7. Upload resume and complete all required fields.
8. Review and submit.

## Known Quirks

- Uses Workday instance `wd5`.
- HPE is the enterprise/server/cloud company (split from HP Inc. in 2015).
- Do not confuse with HP Inc. (HPQ).
- Sponsors H-1B for engineering roles.
- Acquiring Juniper Networks (JNPR); career portal may change.
- Needs verification: exact Workday board name.
