## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to `https://www.oracle.com/careers/`.
2. Use the job search to find positions by keyword and location.
3. Click on a job listing.
4. Click "Apply" to begin the application.
5. Oracle uses its own Oracle HCM Cloud for recruiting.
6. Account creation with Oracle may be required.
7. Upload resume and fill out the multi-step form.
8. Complete screening questions.
9. Submit the application.

## Known Quirks

- Oracle uses its own HCM Cloud product (dogfooding their ATS).
- The portal URL often starts with `eeho.fa.us2.oraclecloud.com`.
- UI is Oracle's modern Redwood design system.
- Some legacy roles may still use Taleo (Oracle's older ATS).
- Oracle is a major H-1B sponsor.
- The form uses Oracle-specific components, not standard HTML.
