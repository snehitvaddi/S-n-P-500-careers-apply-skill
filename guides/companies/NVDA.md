## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to the NVIDIA Workday career site.
2. Search for positions by keyword, location, or category.
3. Click on a job listing.
4. Click "Apply" to start the Workday wizard.
5. Create a Workday account or sign in (global Workday account works).
6. Follow the 7-step Workday wizard.
7. Upload resume on the "My Experience" step (wait for auto-parse).
8. Complete screening questions.
9. Fill EEO fields (default to "Decline").
10. Review and submit.

## Known Quirks

- Uses Workday instance `wd5`.
- Board name is `NVIDIAExternalCareerSite`.
- NVIDIA is a top H-1B sponsor, especially for AI/ML roles.
- Very high volume of applicants; response times may vary.
- Resume parsing in Workday often misformats NVIDIA-specific technical skills.
