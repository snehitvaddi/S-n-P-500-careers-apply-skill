## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to the IBM Workday career site.
2. Search for positions by keyword, location, or business unit.
3. Click on a job listing.
4. Click "Apply" to start the Workday wizard.
5. Create a Workday account or sign in.
6. Follow the standard Workday 7-step wizard.
7. Upload resume and complete all required fields.
8. Review and submit.

## Known Quirks

- Uses Workday instance `wd5`.
- IBM has a massive number of open roles globally.
- IBM is a major H-1B sponsor.
- Some roles may still reference legacy Kenexa (IBM's old ATS) systems.
- IBM careers site has robust filtering by skill, location, and role type.
