## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to `https://www.epam.com/careers`.
2. Use the search/filter to find positions by keyword, location, and skill.
3. Click on a job listing.
4. Click "Apply" to begin the application.
5. Account creation may be required.
6. Upload resume and fill out the application form.
7. Complete screening questions.
8. Submit the application.
