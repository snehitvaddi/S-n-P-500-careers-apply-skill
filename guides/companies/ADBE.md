## Navigation Instructions

1. Navigate to the Adobe Workday career site.
2. Search for positions by keyword, location, or team.
3. Click on a job listing.
4. Click "Apply" to start the Workday wizard.
5. Create a Workday account or sign in.
6. Follow the standard Workday 7-step wizard.
7. Upload resume on "My Experience" step.
8. Complete screening questions.
9. Review and submit.

## Known Quirks

- Uses Workday instance `wd5`.
- Board name is `external_experienced` (separate boards exist for university/intern roles).
- Adobe is a strong H-1B sponsor for engineering and design roles.
- Adobe has separate career tracks for creative and engineering roles.
